Delete Your Tickify Account

You can delete your Tickify account and all associated data directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Tickify app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Scroll down to Account Actions.
  4. Tap Delete My Account.
  5. Confirm by tapping Delete in the dialog.
  6. You will be prompted to re-authenticate with Google to confirm.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Your profile (name, email, photo)
  • All your folders
  • All your task lists and tasks
  • All shared tasks you created or were assigned to
Note: Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone. There is no additional retention period — all data is removed from our servers upon account deletion.
